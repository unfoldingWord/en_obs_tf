 The Open Bible Stories

These Open Bible Stories (OBS) are 50 key stories of the Bible, from Creation to Revelation, for evangelism and discipleship, provided freely for translation and multi-media sharing in any language. In each of these OBS story guides, you will learn basic translation skills as you study, apply, and minister crafted Bible stories in your community and beyond.
